# Supabase Auth Email Templates

Use these in Supabase Dashboard:

1. Open `Authentication` -> `Email Templates`.
2. Pick the template type.
3. Replace `Subject` and `Body (HTML)` with the content below.
4. Save changes.

Notes:
- `{{ .ConfirmationURL }}` is the action link generated by Supabase.
- `{{ .SiteURL }}` and `{{ .Email }}` are provided by Supabase.
- Keep your redirect URLs configured in `Authentication` -> `URL Configuration`.

---
